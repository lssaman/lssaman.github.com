About Me

My name is Samantha Shifflett. I am about to graduate from Virginia Tech in Blacksburg, VA, with three majors: Creative Writing, Professional Writing, and Human Development. Aside from that, I do a lot for the community. I volunteer at a local crisis hotline, work at a group home, and I am also a part of many clubs around my campus that focus on mental health awareness. Mental Health awareness has always been something I have wanted to be an advocate for. When you struggle with mental illnesses and intellectual disabilities it is sometimes hard to find the way to a normal life. Well, I am here to tell you that I may not have the answer, but I will be there for you, no matter what (is that too cheesy?). You all have something that gives you hope, but you find that hope inside yourself. Maybe this website is what you need to find that something.
